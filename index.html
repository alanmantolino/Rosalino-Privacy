Privacy Policy

Rosalino Desktop App

Last updated: June 30, 2026  |  Effective date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rosalino ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our desktop application. We are committed to transparency, data minimization, and full compliance with applicable privacy regulations.

1. Introduction

Rosalino respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the rights you have as a user. By using the Rosalino desktop application, you agree to the practices described herein.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account details — If you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.
  • Preferences and settings — App configuration, theme choices, language preferences, and user-customized settings stored locally on your device.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash reports and diagnostics — Anonymous, aggregated data about app performance, error logs, and system information (OS version, hardware specs) to help us improve stability.
  • Usage analytics — Anonymous data about feature usage patterns, collected only if you opt in. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Device identifiers — A unique, non-reversible identifier generated locally for session tracking and license validation purposes.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

🔒 We explicitly do NOT collect:

Personal messages, contacts, files, or any content you process through the App. We do not access your keyboard input, clipboard, camera, microphone, or file system beyond what is strictly necessary for the App's core functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  1. App functionality — To provide, maintain, and improve the desktop application.
  2. License validation — To verify legitimate use of paid features.
  3. Crash prevention — To identify and fix bugs and performance issues.
  4. Legal compliance — To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Local storage — Most user data (preferences, settings, cached content) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.
  • Cloud sync — If you opt into cloud sync, data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Encryption — All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption.
  • Data retention — Crash reports and analytics data are retained for a maximum of 90 days, then automatically deleted.

6. iOS & App Store Compliance

While Rosalino is a desktop application, we design our privacy practices to meet or exceed Apple's App Store privacy requirements, including:

6.1 Data Collection & Tracking (App Store Review Guideline 5.1.1)

We do not track users across apps and websites owned by other companies. We do not share user data with data brokers or advertising partners for cross-app tracking purposes.

6.2 Required Privacy Labels (App Store Privacy Nutshell)

Privacy Labels Summary

  • Data Used to Track You: None
  • Data Linked to You: Contact Info, Diagnostics (minimal)
  • Data Linked to You for Product Functionality: Purchase History, User Content
  • Data Not Linked to You: Usage Metrics, Performance Metrics

6.3 App Privacy Manifest

We comply with Apple's App Privacy Manifest requirements, declaring all data collection practices transparently. Our manifest declares no data collection for tracking purposes.

6.4 End-to-End Encryption (if applicable)

Where user data is synced via our cloud service, end-to-end encryption may be implemented to ensure only the user can decrypt their data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights under applicable privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and others):

  • Right to access — Request a copy of your personal data we hold.
  • Right to rectification — Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to deletion — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to portability — Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — Object to processing of your data for certain purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw previously given consent at any time.
  • Right to complain — Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: Rosalino.contact@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

  • Legal basis — We process data based on your consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate interest as applicable.
  • Data Protection Officer — Contact: Rosalino.contact@gmail.com
  • International transfers — Any data transferred outside the EU/EEA uses Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or relies on adequacy decisions.

9. CCPA / CPRA Compliance (California Users)

  • We do not "sell" personal information as defined under the CCPA.
  • California residents may request disclosure of categories of personal information collected, types of third parties it is shared with, and specific pieces of data.

10. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Rosalino is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion.
